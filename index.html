FAQ

Is it free?

For Year 1 the platform is free. Tutors may charge, but you can also barter.

How do payments work?

Arrange directly with the tutor for now (cash, bank, Revolut, etc.). Stripe will be added later.

Is it safe?

We verify GCU status manually to start, collect reviews, and remove any bad actors fast.

Do I need an account?

Not yet. You submit a request and we connect you. Accounts come later with Supabase/Firebase.
